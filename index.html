Record Your First Activity

Follow these simple steps to record your first activity on Campus.Life

Step 1: Click on Plus Icon

Navigate to the homepage and click the Plus (+) icon to initiate a new activity recording.

Step 2: Select Your Activity

From the activities list, locate and select 'Surya Namaskar' as your activity type.

Step 3: Choose Your Pace

Select your preferred pace: Slow, Medium, or Fast. Choose based on your comfort level and experience.

Step 4: Start Activity

Click the 'Start' button to begin tracking your Surya Namaskar session.

Step 5: Complete and Save

Once finished, click 'Finish' to save your activity and post it to your timeline.
